# Strategic Business Recommendations

## Objective

Based on analytical findings, the following recommendations are proposed to reduce customer churn and improve retention performance.

---

## Recommendation 1: Reduce Dependence on Month-to-Month Contracts

### Finding

Customers on month-to-month contracts exhibited the highest churn rates.

### Action

* Promote annual contracts.
* Offer loyalty discounts for longer commitments.
* Introduce contract upgrade incentives.

### Expected Outcome

* Improved customer retention.
* Increased customer lifetime value.
* Reduced churn risk.

---

## Recommendation 2: Focus on Early Customer Retention

### Finding

Customers within their first year demonstrated the highest probability of churn.

### Action

* Improve onboarding experiences.
* Launch customer engagement campaigns.
* Provide proactive customer support during the first twelve months.

### Expected Outcome

* Higher retention among new customers.
* Improved customer satisfaction.

---

## Recommendation 3: Identify and Protect High-Value Customers

### Finding

A small percentage of customers generated a significant share of revenue.

### Action

* Create VIP customer programs.
* Offer personalized service packages.
* Monitor customer satisfaction continuously.

### Expected Outcome

* Protection of critical revenue streams.
* Increased customer loyalty.

---

## Recommendation 4: Expand Support Services

### Finding

Customers using technical support and security services showed stronger retention patterns.

### Action

* Promote support-related products.
* Bundle support services into subscription packages.
* Improve customer education regarding available services.

### Expected Outcome

* Increased service adoption.
* Reduced churn probability.

---

## Recommendation 5: Implement Predictive Churn Monitoring

### Finding

Machine learning successfully identified high-risk customers before churn occurred.

### Action

* Deploy predictive scoring within CRM systems.
* Trigger retention campaigns automatically.
* Prioritize outreach efforts toward high-risk customers.

### Expected Outcome

* Reduced customer attrition.
* More efficient use of marketing resources.

---

## Long-Term Strategy

The organization should move from reactive retention efforts to proactive customer intelligence supported by analytics, machine learning, and continuous monitoring.
