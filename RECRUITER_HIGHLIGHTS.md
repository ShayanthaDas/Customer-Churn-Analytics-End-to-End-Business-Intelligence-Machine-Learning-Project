## Business Problem Solved

The objective was to identify customers most likely to leave and provide actionable recommendations to improve customer retention.

The project answers key business questions:

* Which customers are most likely to churn?
* What factors drive churn?
* Which customer segments are most valuable?
* How can the company improve retention?

## Key Outcomes

* Identified high-risk customer segments.
* Built customer risk scoring logic.
* Developed predictive churn models.
* Created executive dashboards for stakeholders.
* Generated actionable retention recommendations.
