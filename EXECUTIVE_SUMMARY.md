# Executive Summary

## Project Title

Customer Intelligence & Churn Prediction Platform

## Purpose

The purpose of this project was to develop an end-to-end analytics solution capable of identifying customer churn patterns, predicting future churn risk, and providing actionable recommendations to improve customer retention and business performance.

## Business Context

Customer attrition is a major challenge for subscription-based businesses. High churn rates reduce customer lifetime value, increase acquisition costs, and negatively impact revenue growth.

Management required a data-driven solution to understand customer behavior and support proactive retention strategies.

## Project Scope

The project covered the complete analytics lifecycle:

* Data Cleaning and Preparation
* Exploratory Data Analysis
* Customer Segmentation
* Revenue Analytics
* SQL-Based Business Analysis
* Advanced Statistical Analysis
* Machine Learning Prediction
* Business Intelligence Reporting
* Executive Dashboarding

## Technologies Used

* Python
* Excel
* MySQL
* PostgreSQL
* Power BI
* Tableau
* XGBoost

## Key Findings

* Month-to-month contracts exhibited the highest churn rates.
* Customers with shorter tenure were significantly more likely to churn.
* Higher monthly charges were associated with elevated churn risk.
* Customers utilizing support-related services demonstrated stronger retention.
* Customer value and churn risk could be quantified through feature engineering and predictive modeling.

## Deliverables

* Cleaned analytical dataset
* Advanced SQL analytics framework
* Customer risk scoring model
* Churn prediction model
* Interactive Power BI dashboards
* Executive Tableau storyboards
* Strategic business recommendations

## Strategic Value

This project demonstrates how organizations can use analytics and machine learning to improve customer retention, optimize marketing efforts, and support long-term revenue growth.
